About Us

Pioneering excellence in plastic packaging solutions since our inception.

Leading the Way in Plastic Packaging

Kushal Plast is a premier manufacturer specializing in Plastic Blow Moulding and Injection Moulding packaging products. With years of experience and a steadfast commitment to quality, we have established ourselves as a trusted name in the industry, delivering solutions that combine durability with aesthetic appeal.

We cater to a diverse range of industries, offering high-quality solutions in HDPE, LDPE, and PET materials. Our product portfolio includes Bottles, Jars, Cans, and bespoke packaging items designed to meet the rigorous standards of our clients.

Whether you require standard packaging or customized designs in specific colors, shapes, and sizes, our advanced manufacturing capabilities allow us to deliver exactly what you need.
